Why Cast U?
Since, I am a person from a modelling background, I personally have faced a lot of problems back then in entering this field of modelling. Problems like financial support, contact with various casting agencies, project opportunities, knowledge of valid or fake project projects etc were some of the biggest threats to me. I found it very difficult to get genuine projects as a fresher during the start of my career. So the idea of Cast U came up, which will help aspiring models to get in touch with genuine casting directors or agencies and vice versa.

What is Cast U All about?
It is a platform for the people who want to make their first step into the modelling world. To make it simpler, it's a platform where aspiring models can get in touch with various casting agencies, directors and find relevant projects for them and at the same time, genuine casting agencies, directors can hire models for their respective projects.
